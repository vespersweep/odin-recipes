Anabolic Protein Ice Cream

This is as close as you'll get to authentic ice cream without all the calories and sugar.

Ingredients:

  • 480 Grams Unsweetened Vanilla Almond Milk
  • 31 Grams Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 5 Grams Zero Calorie Sweetener
  • 5 Grams Vanilla Extract

Directions:

  1. Fill a large mixing bowl with 480 grams of Unsweetened Vanilla Almond Milk, 31 grams Vanilla Whey Protein Powder, 5 grams Zero Calorie Sweetener, & 5 grams Vanilla Extract. Whisk all of these together thoroughly until there are no clumps.
  2. Pour your mixture into ice cube trays, then place in the freezer.
  3. Once frozen, place your ice cream cubes into a blender and blend on high until the mixture smooths out. If your mixture is too dry to blend, add tiny amounts of Unsweetened Vanilla Almond Milk.
  4. There you go! Enjoy your high protein anabolic ice cream! Add some sugar free chocolate syrup or experiment with different protein powder flavors!
